About Me

I am a data professional with 5 years+ experience in the recruitment and e-commerce industries. I have honed expertise in utilizing data analysis tools to extract, interpret, and present data-driven solutions. My passion lies in uncovering trends, optimizing processes, and driving informed decision-making that has positively impacted business strategies within the industry landscape.

I have a strong track record in building high-impact sales performance, finance, and pricing dashboards. I am highly proficient in SQL, Power BI, and Excel, leveraging these tools to create compelling visualizations and conduct in-depth data analysis. Additionally, my experience in market research—leading intelligence-gathering initiatives and spearheading research projects—has been instrumental in shaping competitive pricing strategies and market positioning.

Optimizing Pricing and Promotions for Profitability
(SQL Project)

This analysis leverages SQL to evaluate how different pricing strategies impact the profitability of a grocery delivery business. By examining pricing and promotional data, it identifies the strongest correlations between pricing tactics and profitability, enabling a more data-driven approach to guide the company toward sustainable growth.

Real Estate Perfomance Dashboard
(Power BI Project)

This project utilizes Power BI to track and monitor real estate performance across property types, cities, and agents. It highlights cap rate, ROI %, occupancy rate, rental income, and sales trends across cities, property type, and property conditions to uncover investment opportunities and performance gaps for a real estate company.

Nigerian Food Price Impact Survey
(Market Research Project)

A real-world market survey conducted in June 2024 to assess the impact of rising food price inflation on Nigerian food businesses and uncover the coping strategies most commonly adopted to mitigate its effects. This research involved questionnaire interviews of over 100 food businesses across major cities in Nigeria.

Global Cybersecurity Threat
(Power BI Dashboard)

This Power BI project explores the global impact of cybersecurity threats from 2005 to 2024. It highlights the most affected countries, targeted industries, common user vulnerabilities, and the most deployed defense mechanisms. Through interactive visualizations, the analysis provides insights into trends shaping cybersecurity and its economic implications worldwide.

Finance Management Dashboard
(Power BI Dashboard)

A comprehensive finance dashboard to provide the management team real-time visibility into key financial metrics, including EBIT, revenue, net income, and profit margins. This Power BI dashboard enables management to track financial performance, identify cost-saving opportunities, and make data-driven strategic decisions.
